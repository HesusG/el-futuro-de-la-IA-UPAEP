# Title — v8-fresh-conservative

## Title
**El compañero invisible: cómo los estudiantes universitarios ya transformamos nuestra relación con el aprendizaje a través de la IA**

## Short Title (if character limit applies)
**El compañero invisible: IA y aprendizaje universitario**

## Rationale
- "El compañero invisible" is the central metaphor: AI as the ever-present seatmate that students consult constantly but institutions pretend isn't there.
- "ya transformamos" uses first-person plural and past tense to emphasize this isn't hypothetical — it's already happening.
- "nuestra relación con el aprendizaje" keeps the focus on the student experience, not on technology or policy.
- The title is conservative and academic but has a hook ("invisible") that creates curiosity.
- No jargon, no English terms, no policy language. A student would naturally write this.
