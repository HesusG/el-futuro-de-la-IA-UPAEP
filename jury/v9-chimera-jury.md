### What the Jury Praised

1. **Counter-question mechanism** — "The closest any entry has come to genuine Socratic dialogue with an LLM" (Marcela). Forces authentic reactions, prevents scripted monologue.
2. **Constellation architecture** — Each prompt is independently functional. If P3's Lorenz curve fails, P4's meta-critique is unaffected. "The only entry that treats failure as a design parameter" (Andrés).
3. **"Can't-fail" P2** — Even corporate disclaimers become prosecution evidence. "Anticipatory routing around LLM safety responses shows genuine technical understanding" (Sofía).
4. **RUPTURA DE MARCO (P4)** — "Genuine emotional engineering" (Andrés). The build through 4 prompts of constructed world makes the frame-break matter.
5. **C4 = 9.00 unanimous** — Highest rule adherence consensus of any version.

### What the Jury Criticized

1. **Fictional witness paradox** (Marcela, R4) — The entry argues against AI speaking FOR communities, then invents a Zapotec educator and puts words in their mouth. **FIX: Add a 2-line Fiscal meta-objection in P3 acknowledging this paradox.**
2. **"Planned spontaneity" tension** (Andrés, Diego) — The RUPTURA DE MARCO is pre-conceptualized as a dramatic beat. Can a planned moment of vulnerability be genuine?
3. **Checklist risk** (Diego) — "Every fashionable concern is here — epistemic inequality, indigenous epistemologies, linguistic bias." Is this synthesis or hedging?
4. **Four-register maintenance** (Diego) — Fiscal + Defensor + Juez + student's own voice across 5 prompts is extremely demanding.

### Phase 2 Challenge Questions
1. If counter-questions are generic, how does the student recover without revealing pre-planning?
2. What if Deep Research returns biased sources, reinforcing the inequality being critiqued?
3. Can planned spontaneity in the RUPTURA DE MARCO be genuinely spontaneous?
4. What distinguishes the Fiscal's rhetoric from the Defensor's at the sentence level?
5. What real data feeds the Lorenz curve? Fabricated metrics = technical theater.
6. What if Gemini responds cooperatively (neither confessing nor deflecting) to P2?

If the student has the nerve to be vulnerable in P4, this wins. If they perform vulnerability, it gets exactly this score.
