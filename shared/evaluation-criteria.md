# Contest Evaluation Criteria — Analysis

## What the jury evaluates
1. **La interacción crítica con la IA** — Are you pushing back, questioning, steering?
2. **La relevancia de los prompts para lograr el objetivo** — Do your prompts serve the topic?
3. **La creatividad y el aporte de la interacción** — Is this original? Does it contribute something?
4. **El apego a las instrucciones sobre la entrega** — PDF format, title, no personal data, max 5 prompts
5. **La calidad de la escritura en las consignas y respuestas del participante** — YOUR writing quality, not the AI's

## Strategic implications
- Criteria 1 + 5 = the jury is reading YOUR words more than Gemini's
- Criteria 3 = creativity matters — safe/generic loses to bold/original
- Criteria 2 = every prompt must clearly serve "AI future in LatAm academia"
- Criteria 4 = follow the rules exactly or get disqualified

## Disqualification triggers
- Not meeting participation criteria (must be posgrado profesionalización docente)
- Not following delivery instructions (format, personal data, etc.)
- Non-original interactions (copied/fabricated)
